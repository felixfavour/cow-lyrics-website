ROUND 3; FOURSQUARE CONSTITUTION


1. What is the highest decision-making organ of the Foursquare Church?
a) The Board of Directors
b) The National Executive Council
c) The Zonal Council
d) The National Convention Body

Correct Answer
d) The National Convention Body


2. How many members should the Board of Directors of the church consist of?
a) 5-10 members
b) 7-12 members
c) 9-15 members
d) 10-20 members

Correct Answer: c) 9-15 members
